Simulation today

As you've read, simulations can be used in a tremendous number of applications, including scientific discovery, product planning, and education. Which of the following activities benefit the least from simulation?

Finding a time to meet up with friends to work on a group project.
Right; this task probably doesn't need to be solved for many different contexts.
Forecasting the weather for later in the week.
No; weather simulations are important.
Estimating how the long-term use of a new fertilizer will affect a field and the surrounding area.
No, although this would be difficult to simulate accurately.
Preparing soldiers for combat situations.
No.
Predicting the spread of a contagious virus.
No; this would be quite useful to simulate!
